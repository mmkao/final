About me


Hello, I am Dr. Mily Kao. Aside from teaching, I am an active student organization advisor. I have had the pleasure of coaching various MUN teams, including online ones, in the last eight years.


This website aims to create a stress-free environment for users to learn MUN. It is my hope that users would find the information presented practical, engaging, and easy to implement. This website is also a small endeavor to make MUN more inclusive, by providing free and accessible resources to aspiring delegates who might not have access to training and support. Not every post-secondary institution has an active MUN Club or a faculty member with coaching experience. This is especially so in community colleges and online campuses.


Learning can happen everywhere, both inside and outside the classroom! I highly encourage you to give MUN a try if you haven't. You will learn about the United Nations, how states engage one another on the international stage, useful transferrable skills, and many more!
