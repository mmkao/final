Welcome to How to MUN!


Interested in Model United Nations but don’t know how?

Here is your one-stop resource portal for everything about MUN. You will learn what MUN is, how to prepare for a MUN conference, and the dos and don’ts in the conference. You will also find in this site additional resources to assist in your preparation, links to major regional and national MUN conferences, and asynchronous practice opportunities. While the content is geared toward MUN at the college level, anyone interested in learning about this academic simulation will find the resources useful.
